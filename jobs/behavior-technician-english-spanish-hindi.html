Central Valley, CA On-site Part-time English / Spanish / Hindi

About the Role

As a Behavior Technician at Aspire Spectrum, you will provide 1:1 ABA therapy in the home and community. You’ll work under the supervision of a Board Certified Behavior Analyst (BCBA) to implement behavior intervention plans that build meaningful skills and support family goals.

Responsibilities

  • Implement individualized behavior support plans and data collection procedures.
  • Facilitate play-based learning and daily living skills.
  • Collaborate with caregivers and team members to promote generalization.
  • Maintain professionalism, punctuality, and clear communication.

Qualifications

  • High school diploma or equivalent (Associate’s or Bachelor’s preferred).
  • Experience with children or individuals with developmental differences preferred.
  • Reliable transportation and ability to work in-home settings.
  • Bilingual in English/Spanish/Hindi strongly preferred.

What We Offer

  • Training and supervision toward RBT credentialing.
  • Flexible scheduling.
  • Supportive, mission-driven team environment.
