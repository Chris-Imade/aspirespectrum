What We Offer

Digital Learning Environment

Personalized, self-paced digital training on mobile devices for children with autism to build social, communication, and life skills.

Special Learning

Develop real-life skills with our vast library of social stories, digital avatars, and specialized training modules.

Applied Behavior Analysis

OUR LOCATIONS

Where We Serve

We currently serve cities and towns in these 6 counties of the Central Valley area of Northern California:

Merced: Atwater, Livingston, Los Banos, Gustine, and Dos Palos
Tulare: Tulare, Visalia, Exeter, Porterville, Dinuba and Lindsay
Kings: Armona, Avenal, Corcoran, Hanford, Kettleman City, Lemoore and Stratford
Madera: Madera, Chowchilla, Oakhurst, Coarsegold, and North Fork
Mariposa: Mariposa, Coulterville, Wawona, Midpines, Yosemite Valley and Catheys Valley
Fresno: Auberry, Big Creek, Biola, Burrel, Cantua Creek, Caruthers, Clovis, and Coalinga

We continue to expand our reach to new cities to get our services closer to you!
