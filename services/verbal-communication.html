Facilitating Clear Expression

Promote verbal communication with Aspire Spectrum’s specialized ABA services. Our interventions focus on facilitating verbal expression, enabling individuals to communicate effectively and confidently. We tailor goals and teaching strategies to fit the learner’s profile.

Targets May Include

  • Requesting needs and wants (mands)
  • Labeling and describing (tacts)
  • Answering and asking questions (intraverbals)
  • Speech clarity and fluency strategies
